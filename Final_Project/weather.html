Stockholm Weather

The climate in Sweden is typically temperate in the south of the country. This means it has cold, cloudy winters and cool, partly cloudy summers. For Stockholm in January the daily average maximum temperatures is 1 degrees Celsius (33℉) with the average minimum -4 degrees Celsius (24 ℉), while in June the average maximum is 20℃ (60℉) with a minimum of 10℃ (50℉). The wettest month for Stockholm is September, while the driest month is January.
