Sápmi - Sami life in Sweden

Dec. 9 - Dec. 30

Sápmi is an exhibition about Sami life in Sweden. It’s a story of possibilities and difficulties, power and resistance, rights and unrighteousness. Sápmi also seeks to show how the Sami have influenced the Swedish and the Swedes have influenced the Sami.

Swedish and Anglo-saxon carols

Dec. 19 - Dec. 20

Christmas concert with classical Swedish Christmas songs and Anglo-saxon carols. Performances by singer Karl-Magnus Fredriksson together with Linnékvintetten from Uppsala and ST Jacobs Chamber choir among others.

Swan Lake at Dansens hus

Dec. 9 - Dec. 13

The world's most famous and beloved ballets turned into powerful street dance. Choreograper Fredrik "Benke" Rydman, from the dance company "Bounce". Tchaikovsky's original music is mixed with contemporary songs.
